Nike, Inc. is a global leader in the design, manufacturing, and marketing of athletic footwear, apparel, equipment, and accessories. Founded on January 25, 1964, by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight, the company originally operated as Blue Ribbon Sports before officially becoming Nike, Inc. in 1971. Headquartered in Beaverton, Oregon, Nike is renowned for its innovative products, iconic branding, and the famous "Just Do It" slogan. The company's portfolio includes well-known brands such as Air Jordan, Converse, and Hurley. Nike's commitment to sustainability, community engagement, and empowering athletes worldwide underscores its mission to bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete in the world. If you have a body, you are an athlete.
